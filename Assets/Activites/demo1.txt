<h5>Regular activities of Bengal Cancer Foundation</h5>
<p>Cancer being the second leading cause of death globally has no antecedents for its eradication/control. Bengal Cancer Foundation has undertaken this stupendous task through its sharply defined objectives. Also establishing a Day Care Centre for Cancer Patient at Kolkata is on the cards. Also Cancer Awareness Programs are held regularly by Social Works every month in West Bengal and Bangladesh.</p>

<strong>
Health Check & screening
</strong>
<br>
-    Routine Health Check and cancer health check
-    Personalized medicine consultation
-    Genetic counseling

•    Patient counseling session
-    Counseling
-    Psychiatric management
-    Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation
-    Patient support
-    Palliative care & Hospice support

•    Physiotherapy & rehabilitation support
-    Lymphedema management
-    Neurological management
-    Colostomy care
-    Splint / Prosthesis
-    Wigs / Breast prosthesis
-    Speech therapy & Voice rehabilitation

•    Patient consultations and treatment
-    Regular consultation with oncologists and onco-surgeons
-    Weekly honorary consultation with oncologists from Chennai
-    Tele-medicine consultation
-    Supportive care

Awareness programs
-     Regular awareness programs through direct interaction & media interaction
